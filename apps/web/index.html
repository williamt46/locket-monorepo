FOR THE BUILDERS

Your intimate data is safe.

Here is why.

AES-GCM-256

Local encryption. Your SQLite database is encrypted at rest with a key derived from your biometric.

UMBRAL PRE

Proxy Re-Encryption. You delegate a view, not a copy — no key transfer ever occurs.

FHIR R4

Clinical output standard. Shareable with any EHR, no translation layer required.

HYPERLEDGER FABRIC

Consent anchoring. Every access grant and revocation is recorded on a distributed ledger you do not need to trust us to maintain.

LOCAL-FIRST

Zero server-side plaintext. If our servers were subpoenaed, there is nothing to hand over.

SQLITE + EXPO SECURE STORE

On-device persistence. Your Ledger lives in your phone's protected storage, not a cloud bucket.
