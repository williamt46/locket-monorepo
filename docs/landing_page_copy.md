## 2. Core Features (The Sovereign Experience)

### Section Header: The Weight of Ink, The Precision of Cryptography

**Feature 1: The Daily Inscription (Core)**
No forms to fill. No data fields to submit. Stamping your symptoms or cycle status onto Locket’s pages feels like wet ink on heavy parchment. Your data is instantly inscribed and cryptographically sealed on your local device.

**Feature 2: The Portal View (Pro)**
Grant secure, temporary access to doctors or partners. Open a secure window to your ledger and set the expiration. When the time is up, the locket snaps shut and the seal is restored. Total control remains in your hands.

**Feature 3: The Cloud Mirror (Pro)**
For those who demand redundancy without sacrificing sovereignty. Mirror your ledger to your private cloud with a single tap.


## 3. How It Works (The Migration)

**Section Header:** Bring Your History Forward

**Step 1: Export from Legacy**
Download your data from other health tracking apps like Clue or provide your own spreadsheet files. 

**Step 2: Transcribe to Locket**
Watch as our migration service transcribes your entire history. Old spreadsheets are systematically converted into Locket’s Digital Ink, repopulating and encrypting your ledger in real-time.

**Step 3: Seal the Ledger**
Once inscribed, your history is sealed—private, permanent, and entirely yours. We can’t see your data, even if we wanted to.

*Annotation:*
*This section answers the immediate objection of "I already have years of data in another app" by framing the import process as a beautiful "transcription" into the new ledger.*
